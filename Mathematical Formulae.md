- Instantaneous range to a point on the ground:
$$r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$$
- Range history:
$$\sqrt{r_0^2+(vt)^2}$$
- Angular Range Beamwidth (approx):
$$\Theta_r = \lambda/w$$
- Angular Azimuth Beamwidth (approx):
$$\Theta_a = \lambda/l$$
- Swath Area:
$$Area = \frac{z^2\lambda^2}{cos^2(\theta)~lw{}}=\frac{r^2\lambda^2}{cos(\theta)~lw{}}$$
- Ground Range resolution:
$$\delta_r = \frac{c \tau}{2~sin(\theta)}=\frac{c}{2~bw~sin(\theta)}$$
- Slant range resolution:
$$\delta_r = \frac{c}{2B}$$
- Received echo at the antenna:
$$r(t) = \alpha s(t-\tau) + n(t)$$
- Chirp signal:
$$f(t) = st+f_0$$
- Phase of the chirp (Integral of the angular frequency):
$$\phi(t) = \pi st^2+2\pi f_c t+ \phi_0$$
- Phase of the radar echo:
$$\phi(t)=\frac{4 \pi }{\lambda}r(t)$$
- Doppler frequency shift
$$ f_d=\frac{2vu}{\lambda}=\frac{2v}{\lambda}sin(\theta)~sin(\phi)$$
- Azimuth resolution:
$$\delta_{az} = \frac{l}{2}$$
- Doppler rate (Azimuth Chirp):
$$s = \frac{-2v^2}{\lambda r_0}$$
